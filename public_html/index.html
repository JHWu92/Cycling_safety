Cycling Safety Project

We provide accurate, city-wide cycling safety maps to improve the cycling conditions of cities. Join us!

Two simple step to help us build the clycling safety map for cities!

Participate

We focus on the automatic computation of cycling safety maps (also known as stress or comfort maps) using various types of open and crowdsourced data, such as traffic history data, road condition etc. Your data will help us to train the machine learning model, so that we could use it to automatically detect safety level of road segment in other places and cities. These maps will help for cycling route choice.
