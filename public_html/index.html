Cycling Safety Project

We provide accurate, city-wide cycling safety maps to improve the cycling conditions of cities. Join us!

Two simple steps to help us build the cycling safety map of DC!

The ratings you provide will help us evaluate whether we can automatically compute cycling safety maps without human input (you won't need to rate more videos!) The idea is to use a broad range of features characterizing the road network, including traffic data, road conditions or accidents as a proxy to understand how safe a street can be. The more videos you rate, the more you'll be helping science and cycling safety!

We will raffle twelve $25 gift cards among all participants! The more videos you rate, the higher your chances of winning one. Winners will be notified via email.

Cycling Safety project is designed and lead by the Urban Computing Lab at the University of Maryland with support from the National Science Foundation National Science Foundation. If you have any suggestion or question, contact us at umdcyclingsafety@gmail.com
